Designer. Adventurer. Lifelong learner

I'm Carolina, artist and user interface designer. During the start of my career, I tried Marketing and Recruitment positions, while I was learning skills on the side. As I worked in my personal blog project during this time, I discovered UI design, HTML, and CSS.


My curiosity and interest for UI design led me to research and learn more about this field. As I was intrigued and passionate for creating great user experiences through beautiful user interfaces, I enrolled into Design school. Since then, I've been designing user interfaces for web and mobile.


During my free time, I'm either readingdesign articlesor enjoying a good cup of tea. If you get to know me, you will soon realize my small obsession for sweets, naps, and nature landscapes.


I get inspired by stories through design pieces. I believe each design can tell a story through a combination of typefaces, colours, images, and icons. While also creating an intuitive and simple user experience.

Fun Facts:

I went bungee jumping, white water rafting, and zip linning within the span of a weekend.

I was born in Panama and my parents are Chinese. I've been asked if Panama is in Asia.
