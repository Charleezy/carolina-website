Designer. Adventurer. Lifelong learner

I'm Carolina, artist and user interface designer.

In search of my passion, I tried recruitment and marketing positions while learning skills on the side. This led to a blog project and from there, I played with the aesthetics of the website through CSS code.


As my curiousity and interest grew, I enrolled into Design school. Since then, I've been designing for web and mobile.


During my free time, I'm either reading design articles or enjoying a good cup of tea.


If you get to know me, you will soon realize my small obsession for sweets, naps, and nature scenery.


I get inspired by stories through design pieces. I believe each design can tell a story through a combination of typefaces, colours, images, and icons.

Fun Facts:

I went bungee jumping, white water rafting, and zip linning within the span of a weekend.

I was born in Panama and my parents are Chinese. I've been asked if Panama is in Asia.
